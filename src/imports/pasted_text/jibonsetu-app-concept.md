# JibonSetu — West Bengal Emergency Response Network

Design and build a modern, government-scale mobile app concept called **JibonSetu (জীবনসেতু)**.

The vision is to create the future emergency response system for West Bengal, connecting citizens, ambulances, hospitals, families, and emergency control centers through a single platform.

This is not just an ambulance booking app.

It is a complete emergency healthcare ecosystem designed for both urban and rural West Bengal.

The design should feel like a real government-backed digital public service, combining the simplicity of Uber, the trust of healthcare systems, and the accessibility of modern Indian apps.

# PRIMARY USER FLOW

Citizen opens app.

Taps Emergency SOS.

System automatically:

* Detects location

* Finds nearest ambulance

* Finds nearest suitable hospital

* Sends emergency details

* Alerts family members

* Starts live tracking

The entire process should take under 10 seconds.
